 Context of Campaign and promotional activities

Datura 076 set out with a social media campaign that mainly focused on the social media platform of Instagram, linked with Facebook.The target audience for Datura are more likely to be Instagram users (18-25 College or University students, ) Main reach objective was to get 10.000- 15.000 in a six month period and a following of 1000 people in 12 months period.      

 

 

 

 

 

 

Learning Points

  •     For the brand Datura 076 there are some important learning points based on the communication and media plan. Datura set out with a media plan to get a reach of 15000-20000lnstagram users and in a year to gain 1000 followers on intended target audience was in the age group of 18-25 and living in the area of the city of Breda, The Netherlands.
  •    
  •  What we learned:
  •  
    • Instagram reels is the most effective tool for our brands exposure.  
    •  Very important  to not deviate from color polete of the brand for all social media post, helps the overall consistent look of the brands page.
    •  Word of mouth is effective highly  for the first initial followers of the brand.
    •  Find the most effective hours of a day to post and stay consistent with those times.
    •   Posting   on a Sunday proved to be very ineffective for exposure.
    •  The use of trending  sounds or music in the posts proved to increase the exposure of the post through Instagram.
    •  Reach and Awareness is easy to accomplish with a small paid promotion or high qaulity content.
    • However to get engagement and interactions is quite difficul.
    • Consistency with posts helps a lot with the audience getting to know the brand better.
    •  Research for the best days and times a day to post proved crucial in getting the exposure we wanted
  • In general we learned that having a good planning nd staying consistent with it proved very effective. However luck and also proper research of trends can help a lot with reach. We also learned to create/find a loyal following that is able to participate and engage is quite difficult.          

Future Points

  • If the campaign for the Brand Datura 076 where to continue their would be some important aspects that could change to make the brands reach more effectiv.     
  •  
  •  
    • Influencer marketing , DATURA would benefit from colaborations with social media influencers.    
    • The use of paid advertising, By paying of ads the exposure rate would be more effective .              
    • Instead of posting two Instagram stories a week , Datura 076 would benifit better by posting a story every day, and also posting a Instagram post daily.   
    • Using more engaging content , (survey or asking the followers questions)
    • Giving away a price or experience of the brand to one of its followers
    •  Use of higher quality video for posts.
